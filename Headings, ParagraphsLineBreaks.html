Welcome!

Introduction

Welcome to the Online Course Enrollment page.
This platform allows students to register for courses easily and access learning materials online.

Available Courses

Computer Science

The Computer Science program offers a wide range of courses such as Programming, Data Structures, and Web Development.

Business Administration

Students in this department can enroll in courses like Management, Accounting, and Marketing.

Enrollment Process

Step 1: Login

Students must first log in with their registered email address before proceeding with course registration.

Step 2: Select Courses

After logging in, students can view the list of available courses and choose the ones they want to register for.
A confirmation message will be shown once the enrollment is successful.

Support

For any issues or questions about enrollment, students can contact the support team through the help section.
Assistance is available Monday to Friday during school hours.

Conclusion

The Online Course Enrollment system makes course registration simple, fast, and accessible to all students.
